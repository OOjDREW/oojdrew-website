OO jDREW Examples in POSL

The discount for a customer buying a product is 5 percent if the customer is premium and the product is regular.

	  discount(?customer,?product,percent5):- premium(?customer), regular(?product).
	  premium(PeterMiller).
	  regular(Honda).
